Courses We Offer

We offer offline coaching in Mathematics and Science for JEE and NEET, Mathematics for Classes XI–XII, and strong foundational courses for high school students. Our programs are designed to build clarity, confidence, and long-term success, with online Mathematics courses also available for JEE aspirants.

JEE Program (Mains & Advanced)

Our offline institute in Bilasi Town, Deoghar, Jharkhand offers a comprehensive two-year JEE (Mains & Advanced) program for Class XI and XII students, covering Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, with structured guidance to ensure strong performance in both competitive and board examinations.

NEET Program NEW

The NEET Preparation Programme by Maths Marathon Deoghar for Class 11 and 12 students focuses on in-depth NCERT-based learning in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry, strengthening conceptual clarity, analytical skills, and exam-oriented practice to ensure confident and effective performance in NEET.

JEE & NEET Foundation

The Foundation Programme at Maths Marathon Deoghar is for students from Class 8 onwards, focusing on strong fundamentals in Maths and Science. Emphasizing conceptual clarity, logical thinking, and problem-solving, it prepares students confidently for boards, Olympiads, JEE, NEET, and other competitive exams.

Mathematics for XI and XII

This Programme by Maths Marathon Deoghar for Class 11 and 12 students focuses on complete syllabus coverage for CBSE, ISC, and State Boards, emphasizing conceptual clarity, structured answer writing, regular assessments, and exam-oriented practice to ensure strong performance and confidence in board examinations.

Online program for JEE

The Online Programme enables JEE aspirants anywhere in India to get mentored by expert faculties at Maths Marathon Deoghar. The programme offers concept-driven teaching, live interactive sessions, regular practice, and exam-focused guidance to develop the analytical and problem-solving skills required for national-level entrance examinations.

Our Experienced Faculty

We have a team of experienced and enthusiastic educators who have guided students for over two decades.

Binay Narayan Khaware Sir

B.N. Khaware sir is one of the seniormost and most respected mathematics educators in Deoghar. An ex-faculty of Ramakrishna Mission, Deoghar, he is the founder of Maths Marathon Deoghar. With over two decades of experience mentoring students for JEE preparation, he has guided generations of learners toward strong conceptual understanding . His students today excel across diverse fields, including engineering, civil services, and scientific research.

Pranay Narayan Sir

Pranay sir is a well-known and highly respected Physics educator with over 25 years of teaching experience. Renowned for his clarity of explanation, he has mentored a large number of students and is especially popular for making complex physical concepts simple and engaging. His teaching approach helps students develop strong conceptual understanding while enjoying the learning process.

Braj Bhooshan Sir

Braj Bhooshan sir is an experienced Biology faculty with over a decade of teaching experience across reputed institutes, including Aakash (Nashik) and multiple institutes in Kota. He has mentored a large number of students preparing for NEET and is known for his focused, exam-oriented approach and strong command over biological concepts. At Maths Marathon Deoghar he mentors NEET aspirants as well as foundation batches.

Ashok Sir

Ashok sir is a dedicated Chemistry faculty who has been associated with the field of teaching since a very young age. Known for his friendly and approachable teaching style, he creates an engaging classroom environment that students enjoy and admire. His energy and enthusiasm make learning Chemistry both effective and enjoyable.

Hemant Sir

Hemant sir is a young, energetic, and dedicated educator known for his hardworking approach and innovative teaching methods. About Us

Maths Marathon Deoghar is a premier mathematics coaching institute located in the sacred town of Baidyanathdham, Deoghar. Founded with a clear academic vision, the institute is dedicated to mentoring students through a deep, conceptual, and logical understanding of mathematics. From its inception, Maths Marathon Deoghar has emphasized visualization, reasoning, and clarity of thought, moving beyond rote memorization to cultivate genuine problem-solving skills. For over two decades, the institute has consistently guided students preparing for school examinations and national-level competitive exams, including JEE, towards academic excellence. Over the years, its students have secured admissions to prestigious engineering institutions across India, and its alumni today serve the nation as engineers, civil servants (IAS), and professionals in key government and scientific roles.
